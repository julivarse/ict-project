About Julia

My name is Julia Vargas, and I am a 14-year-old girl.

I was born on January 18, 2011, and I live in Mexico. I go to Churchill School and love staying active and involved.

I really enjoy spending time with my family—my parents, siblings, and cousins—as well as with my friends. I also like studying and doing extracurricular activities.

One of my biggest passions is cooking. I enjoy aerial dance, organizing games with my family, and playing board games—especially because I can be a little competitive! I love video calling my friends and being around people I care about. I’m also very sentimental and enjoy looking at photos.

Even though I love being with others, I also appreciate time alone—it helps me relax and recharge. I’m someone who really enjoys being happy and making the most out of life. I love shopping, doing crafts, and going for walks around the city.

My mom is Spanish and my dad is Mexican. We live in Mexico now, but during the pandemic, we spent some time living in Spain. I love traveling, especially when I get to visit my Spanish family.
